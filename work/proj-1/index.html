An in-depth cradle-to-grave or cradle-to-gate LCA can provide you with a detailed environmental impact assessment of your product or service across a wide array of impact categories. This includes the following reporting characteristics:
A detailed process flow diagram and system construction via matrix algebra where high quality regionally relevant input data is procured across the most important processes in the value chain;
hotspot analysis identifying key areas of your operation that generate environmental impacts;
A sensitivity and/or uncertainty analysis providing an understanding of the influence of variability on your results;
A scenario analysis illustrating how impacts change under several possible product/service value chain alternatives;
An assessment of next step options to prioritise and reduce your environmental footprint honing in on the most relevant impact categories.
